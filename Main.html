About Me

Sharmila Joshi

My journey as the CEO of Sumo World reflects my determination, vision, and commitment to empowering women while delivering top-notch products and services. Inspired by a proposal from Mr. Anill Bijalani, CEO of Sumo Linen in Mumbai, I launched Sumo World in 2010, dedicated to supplying hotel essentials in Nepal. With a master's degree in Science and a passion for interior design, I overcame health challenges to pursue my dream of combining style and comfort in hotel linens. My commitment to quality and customer satisfaction quickly made Sumo World a leading name in Nepal's hotel industry.

Sumo
World

Sumo World is a leading importer and distributor of the finest hotel linens and fabrics. We specialize in bed linens, including bed sheets, pillow covers, bath towels, hand towels, face towels, bath mats, duvets, duvet covers, blankets, and mattress protectors. Our table linen collection features table cloths, table napkins, overlays, kitchen napkins, and multons. Additionally, we offer custom uniforms tailored to client satisfaction for entire team members.

Our wide range of high-quality products, competitive prices, and commitment to excellence have positioned us as one of the fastest-growing companies in Nepal. Based in Kathmandu, we serve the hospitality industry and residential customers with a variety of towels, quilts, bed covers, and other soft furnishing items in vibrant shades, whites, and jacquards.
